LB 191 credits give you 3 extra days off your Tentative Release Date (TRD) each month for good behavior. These days are applied to your Tentative Release Date (TRD) when they are earned.

If you don’t have a mandatory minimum, you can start earning these days after your first 12 months. There’s no limit to how many credits you may build up, and once you have earned them, they cannot be taken away.
If you do have a Mandatory Minimum, you can start earning these credits after serving the mandatory minimum term.
