## What is ‘Opportunities’?

This app helps you track and understand your release dates. It has the same release date information as your COIII.

## What information will I see in this app?

The app will show you information about the release dates that ADCRR has calculated for your sentence. Tap on each date in order to see more information about what it means for you. Note that the information shown in the app can change over time, including through the day of your release – for example, if you get a disciplinary infraction that affects your dates.

## How often does the information update?

If your release dates change in your COIII’s computer system, that change will show in this app 1-2 days later.

## Why do other people see more or fewer dates than I do?

The app shows the dates specific to your own sentence or sentences. Some sentences have more release options than others. For example, a person serving an 85% sentence for burglary may see up to 5 dates in the app. A person serving a flat (100%) sentence for armed robbery may only see 2 (SED / CSED).

## What is Time Comp?

The Time Computation Unit (“TCU”) calculates the release dates for everyone sentenced to ADCRR. When you first arrive, TCU will review your court documents to make sure your sentence is recorded correctly. TCU will recalculate your projected release dates that may impact your earned release credits during your incarceration period.
