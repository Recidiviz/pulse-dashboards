## What is 'Opportunities'?

This app helps you track and understand your release dates. The information updates {update cadence}.

## What information will I see in this app?

The app will show you information about the release dates that ADCRR has calculated for your sentence. Tap on each date in order to see more information about what it means for you. Note that the information shown in the app can change over time – for example, if you get a disciplinary infraction that affects your dates.

## Why do other people see more or fewer dates than I do?

The app shows the dates specific to your own sentence or sentences. Some sentences have more release options than others. For example, a person serving an 85% sentence for burglary may see up to 5 dates in the app. A person serving a 100% sentence for armed robbery may only see 2 (SED / CSED).

## What is Time Comp?

The division within ADCRR who are responsible for calculating sentence dates.
