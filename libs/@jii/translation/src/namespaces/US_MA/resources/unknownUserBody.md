You may be seeing this page for one of these reasons:

**Recent arrival** — If you came into a MADOC facility less than a month ago (whether for a new charge or a violation), **your information may not be in the portal yet**. Tablet accounts for this app are updated by the 12th of the next month.

**Federal or Interstate sentence** — If you were sentenced in a jurisdiction other than Massachusetts but are currently housed in a MA Department of Correction facility in accordance with the Federal and Interstate Compact, **your case is not included in this portal**. Instead, Earned Good Time is calculated by the sending state or Federal authority who will provide you with this information in accordance with their policies and procedures.

If these reasons do not apply to you, you can submit a ticket by tapping on the (?) in the bottom left corner of the screen.
