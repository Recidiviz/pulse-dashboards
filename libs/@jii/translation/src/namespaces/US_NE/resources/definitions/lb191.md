LB 191 credits give you 3 extra days off your Tentative Release Date (TRD) each month for good behavior. These days are applied to your Tentative Release Date (TRD) when they are earned.

If you don’t have a mandatory minimum sentence, you can start earning these days after your first 12 months of incarceration without any disqualifying class one, class two, or more than three class three misconduct reports. There’s no limit to how many credits you may build up, and once you have earned them, they cannot be taken away.

If you do have a Mandatory Minimum sentence, you can start earning these credits after serving the mandatory minimum term without any disqualifying misconduct reports.
